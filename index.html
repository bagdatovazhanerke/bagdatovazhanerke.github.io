About me

My name is Zhanerke, and I am thrilled to have you here. I am an Undergraduate Biological Sciencfes student at Nazarbayev University, Kazakhstan. Ever since I was young, I have been passionate about biology, and I have pursued this passion throughout my life. I believe that hard work always pays off, and I strive to live by these principles every day. Studying biology at one of the best universities in Kazakhstan has helped me develop my laboratory skills. I am always eager to learn and grow, and I believe that there is always room for improvement. Outside of university, I do content creation, copywriting, and SMM. I find that these activities help me relax and recharge, and they inspire me to approach my work with renewed creativity and energy.

My passion for biology was ignited through my involvement with the NU Red Crescent Society.
